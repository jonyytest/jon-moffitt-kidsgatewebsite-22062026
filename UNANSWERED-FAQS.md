## Billing & plans (prospective + current parents)

### 1. What payment methods do you accept?
Cards? E-wallets (GoPay / OVO / DANA in Indonesia, PromptPay in Thailand)?
Bank transfer? This matters a lot in the SE-Asian markets.
> Draft (needs facts): "We accept …. In Indonesia we also support …, and in
> Thailand …."

### 2. Do prices include tax (VAT / GST)?
> No draft — depends on billing setup per market.

### 3. Is there a refund policy?
E.g. pro-rata refunds on yearly plans, a 14-day money-back window, or no
refunds after billing. **Needs a policy decision before publishing anything.**

### 4. Can we pause a subscription instead of cancelling?
> No draft — needs a product/policy decision. Note the activation fee rules
> already published ("only applies again if an account lapses or cancels and
> then rejoins") make "pause vs cancel" a question families will genuinely ask.

### 5. Will my price change after I subscribe?
Is the signup price locked in (grandfathering), or can it rise with notice?

### 6. Is the free trial once per family? Can it be extended?
> Draft (needs confirming): "The 30-day trial is available once per family
> account."

### 7. If we cancel and later rejoin, is progress and are tokens kept?
Interacts with the activation-fee re-charge rule already on the site.
**Data-retention half of this also belongs in the privacy docs.**

## Product & learning (prospective parents, homeschoolers)

### 8. What language are the lessons taught in?
The website UI is in English / Indonesian / Thai / Chinese — but are the
**lessons themselves** English-medium only? Are instructions localised for
young Indonesian/Thai beginners? This is one of the most likely questions in
ID/TH markets and nothing on the site answers it today.

### 9. Which English skills are covered — reading, writing, listening, speaking?
And does the Cambridge English content prepare children for Cambridge YLE
exams (Starters / Movers / Flyers)? Exam-prep is a big purchase driver.

### 10. My child is younger than 5 or older than 12 — can they still use it?
> Draft (needs a decision): "Content spans Grades 1–6. If your child is just
> outside the 5–12 range, contact us and we'll advise honestly whether it fits."

### 11. Does it support children with dyslexia, ADHD or special educational needs?
Sensitive — publish nothing until the team has a vetted, honest answer.

### 12. Monthly prize draws — what are the prizes, who is eligible, where are the official rules?
**Needs legal review, not just copy.** Prize promotions are regulated in
several target markets (notably Indonesia and Thailand). The site currently
explains how entries are earned but nothing about prizes, eligibility, age
rules or terms.

### 13. Can parents restrict or approve what's available in The Kids Gate Store?
> No draft — needs product confirmation.

## Account & technical (current users)

### 14. What are the minimum OS versions and the app download size?

### 15. Can one child use multiple devices — does progress sync?
Lessons are cached for offline use, so parents will assume sync exists.
Needs confirming before we promise it.

### 16. Can both parents (or a grandparent) have their own dashboard login?
> No draft — needs product confirmation.

### 17. How long is data kept after account deletion?
Must come from the privacy documents / DPO decision — **do not draft copy
for this without legal sign-off.**
